Coming 2020!

Baaricon


Baaricon is an event for adults who are interested in different forms of popular culture. It will be held the 24th of November 2018 in 45 Special in the centre of Oulu. Baaricon will offer a great chance to party as well as to have fun and relax with people with similar interests.

The programme will contain cosplay, dance and different kinds of competitions. The event has an age limit, so the programme will reflect that. 45 Special takes care of that nobody will be thirsty or hungry thanks to their delicious drinks and fantastic food.


Baaricon is organised by a non-profit seeking association called Show-cosplay-yhdistys ry.

45 Special


45 Special is located right in the centre of Oulu at Saaristonkatu 12. The restaurant has three floors, which are all accessible during the event. Unfortunately there is no access to an elevator at the restaurant. The basement will be reserved just for dancing. The first floor will be used for quieter activities such as mahjong, board games general hanging. The programme will be held in the second floor.

The kitchen in 45 Special offers snacks, burgers and nachos with reasonable prices. The kitchen will open at 5 pm and will be open through the event. The menu in Finnish can be found via the link below.

Tickets


Tickets for presale will be available in the spring of 2018. Tickets will also be sold at conventions in Northern Finland.

8€ presale

10€ at the door

Please note, that the event has an age limit of 18 years. Tickets can be bought without age indentification, but an identity card will be required at the door.


In addition 45 Special will charge a cloakroom fee on 2,5€.

Accomodation


For those in need of accommodation there are several hotels in the centre of Oulu. We recommend booking well in advance.


BAARICON - Rules and regulations


1: Follow the rules of the event. Security officers may remove any person causing trouble.
2: Identity card is required at the door. Make sure your face is recognisable.
3: The dress code is unrestricted but compulsory. Please note that the bar will be open for other customers at 10 pm.
4: All replica weapons are prohibited.
5: Cosplay outfits are welcomed, but large dresses, armors and props are prohibited.
6: Bringing your own food and or drinks is forbidden.
7: Do not take pictures of any individual without his or her permission.
8: Remember to drink some water in between alcoholic drinks.
9: Take care of yourself and of other attendees. If you see someone passed out call for help.
10: Do not block corridors or toilets.
